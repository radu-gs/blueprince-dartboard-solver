Welcome to the Blue Prince darboard puzzle solver!

Click on the segments set their state.

Valid segments and states:
- score segment: unselected,selected,hatched (hatched segments are considered partially filed)
- inner bullseye: normal, square, double square, diamond
- outer bullseye: normal, wave pairs (1-3),square, double square, diamond
- outer rim mod segments: / , X , dots (2-4), square, double square, diamond

Click on the Color buttons to set the color of each zone.
